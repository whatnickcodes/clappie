## Step 4: Calculate Australian Apparent Temperature

Formula: AT = Ta + 0.33 * e - 0.70 * ws - 4.00

Where:
- Ta = dry bulb temperature in Celsius
- e = water vapour pressure in hPa = (rh / 100) * 6.105 * exp(17.27 * Ta / (237.7 + Ta))
- ws = wind speed in m/s
- rh = relative humidity (%)

For INDOOR sensors: use ws = 0.1 (still air assumption).
For OUTSIDE: use the actual wind_speed_10m converted from km/h to m/s (divide by 3.6).

Round AT values to 1 decimal place.
