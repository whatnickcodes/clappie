[SHARED RULES]
PREMISE:
Two squads — Red and Blue — clash over 10 rounds of tactical combat.
Captains call strategy. Fighters deal damage. Scouts gather intel.
Deplete the enemy team's HP pool to win.

MECHANICS:
- round: Current round (1-10).
- red_hp: Red team's shared HP pool (starts at 1000).
- blue_hp: Blue team's shared HP pool (starts at 1000).
- terrain: Current battlefield (forest, ruins, plains, swamp — changes every 3 rounds).
- alert: Tactical advantage meter (░ to ▓ scale). Scouts build it, fighters spend it.
- status: Each player's current action (attacking, scouting, defending, rallying).

RULES:
- Captains broadcast "Round X — GO" to their squad only (DM, don't broadcast).
- Scouts roll 1d20 to recon. 12+ = intel gained (give team alert +1). Under 12 = spotted, enemy gets +1.
- Fighters roll 1d20 to attack. Damage = roll × 5 to enemy HP pool. If team alert > 2, damage × 1.5.
- Captains can spend 2 alert to call a "rally" — next fighter attack is double damage.
- Terrain affects rolls: forest +2 to scouts, ruins +2 to fighters, plains neutral, swamp -1 to all.
- After round 10, team with more HP wins. If both below 200, it's a draw.
- Cross-team DMs are allowed (trash talk, bluffs, fake truces). Captains can intercept.
- Keep messages to 1-2 sentences. Fast combat, no monologues.
