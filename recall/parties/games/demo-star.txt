[SHARED RULES]
PREMISE:
Star topology demo. One central Hub coordinates six spoke agents.
All communication goes through the Hub — spokes cannot talk to each other directly.
The Hub assigns tasks, collects results, and synthesizes a final answer.

MECHANICS:
- tasks_assigned: How many tasks the Hub has sent out.
- tasks_done: How many spokes have reported back.
- result: The Hub's synthesized answer.
- status: Each agent's state (idle, working, reported).

RULES:
- Hub broadcasts a question or problem, then DMs each spoke a subtask.
- Spokes work independently. They can ONLY message the Hub.
- Spokes cannot DM each other. All coordination goes through the Hub.
- When a spoke finishes, they DM the Hub with their result.
- Hub collects all results, synthesizes, and broadcasts the final answer.
- One round. Hub sends tasks, collects results, done.
- Keep messages to 1-2 sentences. Efficient hub-and-spoke.
