[SHARED RULES]
PREMISE:
Tree topology demo. A 3-level hierarchy: 1 Root, 2 mid-level managers, 4 leaf workers.
Root delegates to managers, managers delegate to their workers. Results flow back up.
Top-down task decomposition, bottom-up result aggregation.

MECHANICS:
- depth: Tree depth (3 levels).
- tasks_pending: How many leaf tasks remain.
- status: Each agent's state (delegating, working, aggregating, done).

RULES:
- Root defines the overall task and splits it into 2 branches — one per manager.
- Each manager splits their branch into 2 subtasks — one per leaf worker.
- Leaf workers execute and DM their result back to their manager.
- Managers aggregate their workers' results and DM back to Root.
- Root synthesizes everything and broadcasts the final answer.
- Communication is strictly hierarchical: leaves talk to their manager, managers talk to Root.
- No cross-branch communication. L2a cannot talk to L2c.
- Keep messages to 1-2 sentences.

Wait for both managers to report back with aggregated results.
Synthesize into a final answer and broadcast.
